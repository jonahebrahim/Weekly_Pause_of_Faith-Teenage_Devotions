Weekly Pause of Faith

Devotion for the Week of 29th June 2025

Month’s Theme: You Are Called Now

God’s not waiting for you to grow up or be perfect. He’s calling you to live with purpose and faith right now — in school, with friends, and in your heart.

This Week’s Focus: You Were Made for Light

Even when the world feels dark, God created you to shine His light. Don’t hide it — let it shine.

Monthly Memory Verse

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord,
“plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
— Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV)

God, please help us remember Your word,
to trust Your plans, and to hold onto hope every day.

This Week’s Bible Passage

“You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden.
Neither do people light a lamp and put it under a bowl. Instead they put it on its stand,
and it gives light to everyone in the house.
In the same way, let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven.”
— Matthew 5:14–16 (NIV)

Devotion

Sometimes, we try to blend in, to not be noticed. But Jesus says something different: you are light. Not “you might be” or “you could be”—you are.

Light stands out in darkness. That can feel awkward or uncomfortable, especially if others around you don’t follow Jesus. But you weren’t made to hide. God created you to reflect His love, truth, and joy wherever you go.

When you show kindness, choose honesty, include someone who’s left out, or even just stand up for what’s right — that’s light. People notice, even if they don’t say anything.

This week, don’t be afraid to be different. That’s what light does. You might not feel like your life shines, but when you follow Jesus with your actions, your light matters. And it might help someone else find their way, too.

Reflective Question

Where is God asking you to shine your light this week? Who around you might need to see His love through your actions?

What Word or Phrase Stands Out to You?

Think about the verse. Maybe “let your light shine” or “cannot be hidden” sticks with you. Keep that phrase in your heart this week.

Memory Verse Reminder

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord,
“plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
— Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV)

Prayer

God, thank You for making me a light. Help me to shine brightly, even when it feels hard or scary. Show me where and how You want me to reflect You this week. Amen.
