Devotion for the Week of 15th June 2025

Month’s Theme: You Are Called Now

God’s not waiting for you to grow up or be perfect. He’s calling you to live with purpose and faith right now — in school, with friends, and in your heart.

This Week’s Focus: God Works Through Your Everyday Faithfulness

It’s not about being famous or powerful. God loves to use your small acts of faith every day — right where you are.

Monthly Memory Verse

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord,
“plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
— Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV)

God, please help us remember Your word,
to trust Your plans, and to hold onto hope every day.

This Week’s Bible Passage

“Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much...”
— Luke 16:10 (NIV)

Devotion

Have you ever felt like the things you do don’t matter much? Like praying alone, doing chores, or helping a friend quietly isn’t important enough? Jesus tells us something really cool — if you can be trusted with little things, you can be trusted with big things too.

That means the way you live every single day — when no one is watching — is actually a big deal. God sees the small choices you make: choosing kindness, being honest, standing up for what’s right, even when it’s hard. These little choices show that your heart is faithful.

And when God sees that kind of heart, He loves to use it. He doesn’t wait for you to be the best speaker or the leader of a crowd. He uses you *right now*, just as you are — especially when you stay faithful in the little things.

So don’t underestimate your small moments. A quiet prayer, a kind word, or even finishing your homework with integrity — it all matters to God. These are the seeds of faithfulness He will grow into something amazing.

This week, let’s ask God to help us be trustworthy in the small stuff. Because with Him, nothing done in love is ever wasted.

Reflective Question

What’s one small thing you can do today with love and faith? How might God use it in a bigger way?

What Word or Phrase Stands Out to You?

Take a moment to notice if any part of the memory verse or this week’s Bible passage speaks most clearly to you. Write it down or remember it.
