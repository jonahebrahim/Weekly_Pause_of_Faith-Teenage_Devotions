Weekly Pause of Faith

Devotion for the Week of 22nd June 2025

Month’s Theme: You Are Called Now

God’s not waiting for you to grow up or be perfect. He’s calling you to live with purpose and faith right now — in school, with friends, and in your heart.

This Week’s Focus: God Works Through Your Weakness

God doesn’t need you to be perfect or powerful. In fact, He often does His best work through the places where you feel the weakest.

Monthly Memory Verse

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord,
“plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
— Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV)

God, please help us remember Your word,
to trust Your plans, and to hold onto hope every day.

This Week’s Bible Passage

“But he said to me, ‘My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.’ Therefore I will boast all the more gladly about my weaknesses, so that Christ’s power may rest on me.”
— 2 Corinthians 12:9 (NIV)

Devotion

Do you ever feel like you’re not enough — not smart enough, not confident enough, not good enough? The truth is, we all have weak spots. But that doesn’t mean God can’t use you. Actually, it’s the opposite!

Paul, who wrote this verse, had something he really struggled with. He asked God to take it away, but instead, God gave him something better — a reminder that His grace is enough, and His power shines brightest when we are weak.
