Weekly Pause of Faith

Devotion for the Week of 1st June 2025

Month’s Theme: You Are Called Now

God’s not waiting for you to grow up or be perfect. He’s calling you to live with purpose and faith right now — in school, with friends, and in your heart.

This Week’s Focus: God Is Close When It Hurts

Pain, loneliness, pressure — God doesn’t run away from these. He draws near.

Monthly Memory Verse

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord,
“plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
— Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV)

God, please help us remember Your word,
to trust Your plans, and to hold onto hope every day.

This Week’s Bible Passage

“The righteous cry out, and the Lord hears them;
he delivers them from all their troubles.
The Lord is close to the brokenhearted
and saves those who are crushed in spirit.
The righteous person may have many troubles,
but the Lord delivers him from them all;
he protects all his bones,
not one of them will be broken.”
— Psalm 34:17–20 (NIV)

Devotion

This week’s passage reminds us that being a follower of God doesn’t mean life will be easy or free from troubles...

Reflective Question

What is one struggle or worry you want to bring to God this week? How can knowing He is close change the way you feel about it?

What Word or Phrase Stands Out to You?

Take a moment to notice if any part of the memory verse or this week’s Bible passage speaks most clearly to you. Write it down or remember it.

Memory Verse Reminder

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord,
“plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
— Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV)

Prayer

God, thank You that You hear me and are close when I’m hurting. Help me trust Your plans and find peace even in hard times. Amen.
