Weekly Pause of Faith

Devotion for the Week of 1st June 2025

Month’s Theme: You Are Called Now

God’s not waiting for you to grow up or be perfect. He’s calling you to live with purpose and faith right now — in school, with friends, and in your heart.

This Week’s Focus: God Is Close When It Hurts

Pain, loneliness, pressure — God doesn’t run away from these. He draws near.

Monthly Memory Verse

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord,
“plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”
— Jeremiah 29:11 (NIV)

God, please help us remember Your word,
to trust Your plans, and to hold onto hope every day.
